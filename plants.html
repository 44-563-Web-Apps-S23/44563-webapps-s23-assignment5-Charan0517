Tamarix

Tamarix is a small shrub or tree marked by slender branches, tiny, grayish green leaves, and pale pink or off-white blooms. Tamarix reaches heights of up to 20 feet (6m), although some species are much smaller. Read on for more Tamarix information.

Ilex

Best known for their brilliant berries in the fall and winter, Ilex plants provide exceptional color and interest in the late season garden. Cut branches of berries from the Holly bushes are prized for Thanksgiving and holiday arrangements. The berries left uncut attract winter birds and returning robins. Kalanchoe

Kalanchoe is popular with gardeners for a variety of reasons. Houseplant growers enjoy this succulent for its long-lasting brilliant red, pink, orange or white bloom heads that appear above the waxy green leaves in the cold of winter. That's when we need colorful flowers the most!
